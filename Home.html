String Art Portraits

Capture Memories in String

Our custom string art portraits transform your cherished photos into unique works of art. Using specialized techniques, we recreate facial features, expressions, and emotions with carefully placed strings.

Each portrait is a labor of love, taking between 20-40 hours to complete depending on complexity. We work with you to select the perfect color palette that matches your home decor and captures the essence of your subject.

Perfect for family portraits, pet memorials, wedding gifts, or celebrating special milestones.

String Art Typography

Words Woven in Art

Our typography collection transforms meaningful words, names, and quotes into stunning string art pieces. Each letter is meticulously crafted with precision and attention to detail.

Choose from a variety of fonts and styles - from elegant scripts to bold modern typefaces. We can create monograms for weddings, inspirational quotes for home offices, or family names for your entryway.

All typography pieces are fully customizable in terms of size, colors, and wording. Perfect for personalized gifts that will be treasured for years to come.

String Art Paintings

Where String Meets Canvas

Our painting collection represents the intersection of traditional art forms with contemporary string techniques. These pieces blend color, texture, and form in unique ways that can't be achieved with paint alone.

Using specialized techniques, we create depth, movement, and emotion through the strategic layering of colored strings. Each piece tells a story through its intricate patterns and carefully chosen color palettes.

From abstract expressions to realistic landscapes, our string art paintings add a sophisticated, textural element to any space. Each piece is a one-of-a-kind creation that can't be replicated.

Our Story

Handcrafted with Passion

Hi, I'm Sarah, the artist behind Arteva. What started as a hobby in my college dorm room has evolved into a passion for creating unique, handcrafted string art pieces.

Each piece is meticulously crafted using sustainably sourced wood and high-quality embroidery thread. I take inspiration from nature, geometry, and personal stories to create art that brings warmth and character to any space.

My mission is to transform simple materials into captivating works of art that tell a story and create an emotional connection with their owners.
